This website was made to teach people about storage devices and media, so that people can get a better understanding of this specific aspect in the devices that are beginning to become more and more common in people households everyday. It is important for people to know this information because these are devices that everyone uses but still don't really understand what makes trhem work and how they work, so this information will help them gain a wider range of knowledge on important information.
